01 — MISSION

The grid ends at the fence line.

AI is driving the largest infrastructure buildout in recent history, and the power grid can't keep pace — in major markets, connection lead times for new data centers run five to seven years. Some companies are pointing rockets at the problem and hauling compute to orbit — chief among them Cowboy Space Corp., the orbital data-center venture founded by Robinhood co-founder Baiju Bhatt, whose project inspired this one.

Ours is smaller and verifiable: put compute where the sun already lands, off-grid, and publish the receipts. No renders of satellites. A panel, a battery, a node, a chain.

02 — PROOF OF SUNLIGHT

From the panel to the chain.

Proof of Sunlight is a small telemetry contract on Robinhood Chain. Every ten minutes, each node signs a reading and posts it. No oracle committee, no middleman dashboard — the node speaks for itself, and the explorer is the audit.

STEP / 01

Measure

The INA226 sensor samples solar input, battery state and bytes served, straight from the wire — not from a self-reported config file.

STEP / 02

Sign

The node's onboard key signs the epoch. One key per steer, its address published on this page — spoofing a reading means stealing the physical box.

STEP / 03

Post

The signed epoch lands in the Proof of Sunlight contract on Robinhood Chain. Ten-minute cadence, gas measured in fractions of a cent.

07 — FAQ

Straight answers.

Is the hardware real?

LONGHORN-01 is in build — panel-class hardware, not a render. Until it posts epoch #1 on-chain, every number on this site is labeled simulated, and we won't blend simulated and real data. The bill of materials is published as it's purchased.

Do I need to connect a wallet?

No, and you never will on this site. Access is verified by pasting your address for a read-only balance check against the public RPC. Nothing to sign, no approvals, no transaction prompts. If any page ever asks you to connect a wallet claiming to be us, it's fake.

Why Robinhood Chain?

It's an EVM chain (id 4663) with near-zero gas — cheap enough to post telemetry every ten minutes without thinking about it — and it's where we already build and operate. Explorer: robinhoodchain.blockscout.com.

Is this affiliated with Robinhood, or Cowboy Space?

No. Solar Ranch is an independent project. We openly took inspiration from Cowboy Space Corp. — the orbital data-center company founded by Robinhood co-founder Baiju Bhatt (formerly Aetherflux) — but we are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Cowboy Space, Robinhood Markets, or the operators of Robinhood Chain in any way. Their compute goes to orbit; ours stays firmly on the ground.

Is $RANCH an investment?

No. $RANCH is an experimental access token: it gates services, nothing more. It can go to zero. Nothing on this site is financial advice, and "Corporation" in the name is branding — the project is currently unincorporated.
